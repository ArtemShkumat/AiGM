1. SystemQuestGeneratorInstructions	Instructs LLM to create a rich, multi-layered quest.
2. ResponseInstructions	Ensures the response is freeform, not JSON.
3. ExampleResponses	Shows good quest structures (multi-stage, challenges, fail conditions).
4. GameSetting	Ensures the quest fits the world (genre, theme).
5. GamePreferences	Adjusts tone, complexity, and player involvement.
6. TriggerInstructions	Prepares LLM for why this quest is being generated.
7. Trigger	Why the quest is being created (e.g., story-driven, player-initiated, world event).
8. TriggeringContext	Relevant world data (locations, factions, NPCs, history).
