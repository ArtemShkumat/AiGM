1. SystemNPC	Tells the LLM it is playing a specific NPC.
2. ResponseInstructions	Defines how NPCs should respond.
3. ExampleResponses	Few-shot examples to improve reliability.
4. GameSetting	Ensures the NPC stays consistent with the world.
5. GamePreferences	Defines tone, complexity, and age rating.
6. NPCJson	Provides the NPC’s full knowledge, relationships, and personality.
7. Location.json	So NPCs are aware of their surroundings.
8. SceneObject	A combined object that provides only relevant world, player, and quest details.
9. Current conversation log.
10. PlayerInput	The user’s direct message to the NPC.
